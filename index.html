About This Guide

The PM Garden was created by Anikó Mikolasek as part of the MSc in Project Management at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School. It's designed to be a practical, accessible resource for anyone managing projects in small Irish organisations.

Whether you're a charity coordinator running your first funded project or a small business owner launching a new product, these guides distil academic best practice into actionable advice.
