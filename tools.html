Tools & Recommendations

Curated project management tools for small businesses and charities

Choosing the right tools can make project management significantly easier, especially for small teams with limited budgets. Here are our recommendations, tested and reviewed for Irish organisations.

T
Trello
Task Management
★★★★★Ease of use

Trello's kanban-style boards are perfect for visualising project tasks. Drag cards between "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done" columns. Incredibly intuitive, teams can be productive within minutes of signing up.

Free tierKanbanMobile appIntegrations
Best for: Small teams who want visual task management with minimal setup
Visit Trello →
A
Asana
Project Management
★★★★☆Feature depth

Asana offers more structure than Trello, with list views, timeline (Gantt-style), calendar, and board views. Great for managing multiple projects simultaneously with dependencies and milestones.

Free for ≤15Gantt viewTemplatesAutomation
Best for: Teams managing multiple projects who need timeline and dependency tracking
Visit Asana →
P
Microsoft Planner
Task Management
★★★★☆Integration

If your organisation already uses Microsoft 365, Planner integrates seamlessly with Teams, Outlook, and SharePoint. Kanban boards with bucket organisation, charts for progress tracking, and familiar Microsoft interface.

Included in M365Teams integrationCharts
Best for: Organisations already using Microsoft 365 who want built-in PM tools
Visit Planner →
M
Monday.com
Work OS
★★★★☆Flexibility

Monday.com is a highly flexible work operating system that can be customised for any workflow. Visual dashboards, automation rules, and recently added AI features make it powerful for growing teams.

Free for ≤2DashboardsAI featuresAutomations
Best for: Teams who need highly customisable workflows and visual dashboards
Visit Monday.com →
M
Miro
Visual Collaboration
★★★★★Collaboration

Miro's infinite whiteboard is perfect for brainstorming, stakeholder mapping, SWOT analysis, and retrospectives. Real-time collaboration means remote teams can work together as if in the same room.

Free tierWhiteboardTemplatesReal-time
Best for: Visual thinkers, remote workshops, and collaborative planning sessions
Visit Miro →
C
Canva
Design & Presentations
★★★★★Ease of use

Canva democratises design for non-designers. Create professional presentations, reports, social media graphics, and project documentation without any design skills. AI-powered features speed up creation.

Free tierTemplatesAI featuresBrand kits
Best for: Creating professional project presentations, reports, and marketing materials
Visit Canva →
